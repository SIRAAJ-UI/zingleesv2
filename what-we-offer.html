What We offer?

Through our services, we aim at enabling schools, teachers, and parents to get access to complete insights on each child to give them the special care and attention that they truly need. We're revamping the entire educational assessment system to the specific needs of each child to ensure that they get the most out of their education.

We Provide the following
Accurate assessments

Through our assessments, we can accurately identify weak subjects and we adapt our subject teachings and assessments accordingly to make the learning process more efficient.

Learning Process

We can take over the learning process by leveraging the expertise of our team of onboard teachers and professionals. Allow us to train your teachers and provide them with modern strategies to maximize their potential.

Generate assessments

Parents and teachers can generate assessments online in subjects and chapters of their choosing.

Experienced evaluation

You can get all assessments evaluated through our experienced group of evaluation partners.

We Provide the following
Learning Platform

Zinglees has created an effective proprietary intelligence solution that will help the schools and teachers create assessments which will help them understand the understanding of every student in every class in every subject in every concept. This simple to use evaluation platform will help you migrate from the current rote memory assessment to purposeful effective learning analysis exercises. The identified learning gaps or learning needs of every student at the end of the evaluation will be critical information for the teachers to customise their teaching to ensure learning for their students.

The solution squarely falls in line with the expectations set by NEP 2020.
Competitive Examinations Practice Platform.

Zinglees has huge volume of questions, answers and solutions for all the competitive examinations for students in class VI to Class XII.
Olympiads, NTSE, KPVY etc for middle and high school students NEET, JEE etc , for Higher secondary students.
Students will have unlimited access to never before flexible practice platforms which will give an insight of their strengths and the areas that they got to focus to perform in their respective competitive examinations.
Practice not just on the academics but also on GK, Aptitude, Logical reasoning etc.

Returns on Instructions

The Zinglees platform will provide detailed insights to the school management and the teachers on their progress/performance and will help them to plan on improving the outcome of their hard efforts.
Management and Teachers will be able to make decisions driven by data and keep monitoring their performances with unlimited repetitive evaluations.
Teacher feedbacks are one of the important aspects that is missing in the current system.
Return on Instructions is the key for return on investment

Data Analytics

Zinglees platform provides you with detailed analytics about the student performance and the teacher performance at subject level and concept level.
The teachers and the management will be able to visually track the progress of every student in every concept throughout the academic year.
The analytical reports will be handy to exhibit to the parents about the kind of efforts that the school and the teachers invest and the learning that the child has achieved.
