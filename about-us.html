About us

ZingLEES (Zing Learner Evaluation & Empowerment Solutions) is proprietary offering of Brain Scale Innovations designed and developed to support teachers and schools in overcoming their practical challenges in the process of ensuring the learning for every child in their classroom. ZingLEES supports teachers and schools in moving from the current practice of examination systems to a more purposeful evaluation systems. ZingLEES also enables the teachers and schools to address and enable every student according to their specific needs, strengths and weaknesses.

Key offerings

With the integration of cutting-edge technology, ZingLEES enables you to take your teaching and learning practices to the next level. With features like personalized learning, exclusive interventions, big data analysis and competitive excellence, you can leverage the power of technology to enhance the learning experience of your students and create a more impactful and effective teaching environment.

Zinglees’ mission

To support schools in moving from the current formal examination system to a purposeful evaluation system, where the instructor/teacher is assisted with reports which will give them a clear picture of the understanding of every child. These services are also available to parents/guardians who desire to get an independent external evaluation done for their child.

To support schools with class-wise, subject-wise Learning Achievement Plans. It has to be understood that the new deliverables defined by the regulatory bodies demand a new pedagogy to achieve them in full. We have structured and organised ‘Learning Achievement Plans’ with effective and innovative teaching of every concept and covering the smallest detail. These plans focus on grooming the competence of every child in every concept in every subject and not just on marks

To support schools in all their technological needs and dependencies. Technology has begun to play a critical role in every sector including education. Any new solution inside or outside school premises must be built around technology for today’s students. Zinglees supports schools either with readymade open-source or paid solutions available or customises them to the needs of the schools.

We strongly believe that we are results of the past and emissaries of the future. Our future is the generation which is now in schools. It is our responsibility to ensure access to quality and equitable education in the interest of every child and the nation as a whole
